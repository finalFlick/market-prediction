# FEATURE-0036: Notifications and Seven Operational Alerts

## Parent Epic

observability-audit-operations

## Status

Proposed

## Summary

Define severity routing and the seven required operational alerts across UI, Discord, and email phases.

## Why

Safety and degraded-data events must reach the operator quickly through appropriate channels.

## What

A notification capability covering alert inventory, severity, routing, suppression, escalation, and UI fallback behavior.

## User / System Story

As a platform operator or implementation agent,
I want notifications and seven operational alerts,
so that the trading-lab platform can satisfy the related requirements with evidence.
